About the event

  1. 1 Aim

    This event aims to motivate students to plan and develop the creative ideas that the students always thought of but lacked the confidence to pursue

  2. 2 Procedure

    There are 2 stages. Ideation where you present your ideas and Implementation where you will code your idea into existance

  3. 3 Learning

    Throughout the both stages possible expert guidence will be provided. All you need is will to learn and grow
